QA Course Overview

App2Automate Quality assurance (QA) Training that helps participants to understand the core concepts of Software Testing and Information Technology that helps them excel as a Quality Assurance Professional. QA Course also covers physical products in pre-production, determining defects in manufactured products and preventing them, during manufacturing, and production, Sandbox Environment Vs Production Environmen, SDLC-Software Development Life Cycle, Overview of Mantis Bug Tracker.
